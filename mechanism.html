Chapter I: Who are these Goddesses?

Beneath the clamor of the real world lies another layer of truth, invisible to the naked eye. Just as the most profound starlight only reveals itself in the fleeting moment of an eclipse—when the sun's blinding rays are obscured by shadow—there exists a realm suspended between reality and fantasy. Standing within this shadow are 48 powerful entities. We call them The Goddesses of Eclipse.

Who are they?

They were not born as untouchable deities. Long ago, they walked the mortal realm just like you and me. They experienced the raw reality of life and death, tasted the heartbreak of betrayal, and survived the abyss of despair. Or perhaps, their souls simply burned with an innate, extreme, and uncompromising nature. These profound marks of suffering and their uniquely forged souls created a resonance powerful enough to shake the stars.

At the dawn of the universe, there were 48 primal cosmic forces. Because the formless laws of the universe require a tangible vessel, these 48 forces chose their souls to become the Avatars of the Cosmos. Today, they live incognito among us. Yet, beneath their mortal guise flows a divinity capable of overturning reality itself.

Why do they guide you?

While traditional tarot depicts abstract fate, the Tarot of Eclipse depicts forged souls. They understand human fragility, greed, fear, and desire better than any high god ever could. When you draw a card, you are not reading a cold prophecy; you are engaging in a spiritual dialogue with a Goddess who has walked through the valley of the shadow of death and was ultimately crowned by the universe.

The Goddesses are ready to reach out to you from the shadows. This deck shall be your bridge to their world of "Arcalis."

Chapter II: The System of the Deck

When you hold the Tarot of Eclipse, you will realize it is unlike any traditional deck. It abandons convoluted frameworks, refining itself into exactly 48 cards. Every card represents the true form and soul imprint of a Goddess. To accurately decode their messages, this deck utilizes a Dual Interpretation System.

I. The Microscopic System:
The Mortal Trajectory

Focuses on the "Human"—the experiences of flesh and blood.

  • Cards 1–24 (Progression): Represents the "process". The growing pains, setbacks, and wisdom accumulated. "What phase of life are you currently in?"
  • Cards 25–48 (Direction): Represents the "choices". Decisions made at crossroads and the ultimate trajectory. "Where is your specific goal?"

II. The Macroscopic System:
The Abstract Cosmic Laws

Focuses on the "Divine"—elevating to a universal height.

Focuses on the abstract concepts these Goddesses represent after being chosen by the cosmos. They embody pure laws maintaining the balance of the universe (e.g., "Silence", "Frenzy").

It reveals what cosmic concept you must introduce to break a stalemate and restore balance.

Chapter IV: Dos, Don'ts, and Warnings

Dos (Follow the Laws)

  • Respect the Reading: Accept the revealed truth entirely with a humble mind. Every arrangement serves the balance of the universe.
  • The Law of Silence: This is the absolute iron rule. If you draw Karnilla, Scarlet Witch, Raven, or Clea, you must stop the divination immediately.
  • Refrain from Re-asking: If the Law of Silence is triggered, you are strictly forbidden from asking the same question again for at least one lunar cycle.

Don'ts (Taboos & Boundaries)

  • Do Not Harbor Disrespect: Divination is not a game. A mocking heart will disrupt the energy field of Arcalis, resulting in backlash-inducing messages.
  • Do Not Force a Reading: Never force a Reader to perform when their mental state is poor. Never force a Querent to draw if they are not ready to face the truth.

The Final Caveat: The Master of Fate

"The Goddesses will only offer you advice; your life is created by you. They cannot bear the physical pain of your flesh, they cannot weep your tears, and they cannot live this brief, precious life for you... The Zenith of Arcalis may be infinitely high, but it is never higher than the brilliance of human free will."
