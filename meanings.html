The Mechanism

I. Who are the Goddesses of Eclipse?

They are not mere archetypes; they are the Queens of the Cosmos. Forged from the raw energies of the universe, each Goddess represents a singular, uncompromising aspect of reality.

When the sun and moon align to cast the world into shadow, the veil between our realm and theirs thins. The Goddesses step forward through the eclipse, offering their ancient sight to those brave enough to ask. They do not judge; they only reveal.

II. The Components of the Deck

The deck consists of exactly 48 cards, cleanly severed into two opposing forces that balance the universe.

The Sequence (1-24)

The domain of order, predictability, and linear progression. These cards represent the foundational laws of nature and society. When drawn, they speak to the structures in your life, the paths already laid out, and the logical consequences of your actions.

The Wild Cards (25-48)

The domain of absolute chaos, profound evolution, and the great unknown. These entities defy the rules. When drawn, they signify sudden shifts, emotional upheavals, hidden motives, and the terrifying freedom of the void.

III. How to Use This Deck

01

Clear the Fog

Before drawing, sit in silence. Visualize your mind as a clouded forest, and allow your breathing to slowly dissipate the fog until you can hold a single question clearly in your mind.

02

The Daily Draw

For daily guidance, draw a single card. Read the Divine Guidance not as an absolute future, but as the energetic current flowing through your specific day.

03

Observe the Duality

Every Goddess embodies a Concept (e.g., Creation, Illusion, Atrophy). You must ask yourself: "Am I currently lacking this concept, or am I drowning in it?"

IV. Sacred Practices (Dos & Don'ts)

DO

  • Approach the reading with a calm, neutral mindset.
  • Ask open-ended questions (e.g., "What should I focus on?").
  • Trust your first instinct when looking at the Goddess's face.
  • Use the cards for self-reflection and empowerment.

DO NOT

  • Draw cards while in a state of extreme panic or anger.
  • Ask the exact same question multiple times hoping for a better card.
  • Use the deck to pry into the private lives of others.
  • Treat the Guidance as an inescapable, doomed fate.

V. The Final Warning

"The Goddesses of Eclipse hold no allegiance to your desires. They act as a flawless mirror. They will not show you the path you wish to walk; they will expose the path you are currently standing on. To open this deck is to forfeit the comfort of your own illusions. Proceed only if you are prepared for the absolute truth."
